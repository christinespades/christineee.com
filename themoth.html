i felt the weight of your first transgressive glance pressing against my ribs.
it could never withdraw its grasp.
the shelter of this moment shattered into irretrievable dust.

we were a failing organ, a fused unit, an organism slowly dying.
a pulse was quickening beneath the skin of our shared life.
a quiet theft was pulling you further into a world i could not reach.
